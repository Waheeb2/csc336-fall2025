Foods That I’ve Had & Places I’ve Been To


Waheeb A. CSC-336

I’ve been fortunate enough to travel to and live in many incredible places. With that, I’ve been
able to experience different cultures, meet amazing people, and my favorite, try different kinds of
foods. Here I’ll be sharing some of my favorite spots that I’ve visited/lived in alongside the
unique foods I was able to try there.

1. Brasilia, Brazil

First on my list is Brazil, which is where I spent most of my childhood. There I made a lot of
unforgettable memories and ate various foods. During my stay there I really enjoyed going to their all you
can eat Brazilian steak restaurants. My two favorite items that I vividly remember enjoying were the
Pão de queijo, which is a Brazilian cheese bread, and the picanha steak.

2. Athens, Greece

My second place on my list is Greece. The country itself is filled with rich history and I believe that
resonated with me more than their food. While there were some foods that I liked, places like the Acropolis, a
historical landmark most notably known for the Parthenon, and the island of Santorini known for its white
blue-tipped buildings, just stood out to me and made my entire stay there one-of-a-kind.

3. Jeddah, Saudi Arabia

Finally on my list is my home country Saudi Arabia. Just like Brazil, I’d enjoy going out with friends and
family to local restaurants that serve Saudi staples like mandi, which is just rice and a choice of protein, and
murtabak, a pan-fried flatbread. For me, out of all the places I've been to, I can always count on Saudi Arabia
for delicious foods.
